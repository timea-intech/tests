Earn Your Degree at the Boston Architectural College

Discover your passion for design at the BAC!

The Boston Architectural College is an independent, nonprofit design studies and architectural college with 125 years of experience training design professionals through an innovative approach to education. Experiential learning and professional practice are built into our curriculum, and our philosophy of open admissions promotes diversity in a traditionally exclusive field and builds a vibrant community on campus.

Our fully accredited graduate and undergraduate programs include a unique selection of design studies and architecture degree programs, allowing students to pursue degrees in architecture, interior architecture, landscape architecture, and design studies with concentrations in historic preservation, sustainable design, design for human health, and more. We provide our students with the tools, skills, and connections needed to impress future employers through our comprehensive design studies and architecture degree programs.

Work While You Learn

The Boston Architectural College distinguishes itself as the only accredited design and architectural college that requires its students to complete discipline-specific employment alongside their coursework. Students acquire vital professional skills and knowledge through hands-on projects and real-world employment, emphasizing collaboration, critical thinking, and community engagement.

The graduates of our design studies and architecture degree programs are accomplished designers with impressive resumes, portfolios, and professional networks.

Learn from Experts in the Field

Since 1889, the Boston Architectural College has approached design education uniquely. Students are taught, mentored, and advised by over 300 practicing professionals, strengthening the connection between workplace experience and classroom learning.

We employ architects, interior designers, landscape architects, and other design-related experts who offer students an excellent design education grounded in the practicalities of real-world experience.

Immerse Yourself in Boston's Design Community

The Boston Architectural College's campus is located on the famous Newbury Street in the heart of Boston, surrounded by inspiring architecture, museums, performance venues, and colleges and universities.

The BAC provides you with more than just the typical architecture degree. The real world application of your academic coursework and close connections to the design community and network of hundreds of design and construction-related companies give our students a distinct advantage in their fields.
