  • Deposit Insurance

    READ MORE

    All deposits at Crescent Credit Union offer two levels of insurance protection. Deposits are federally insured up to $250,000 by the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA), an agency of the federal government. In addition, deposits in excess of $250,000 are insured by the Massachusetts Share Insurance Corporation (MSIC). All IRA accounts are separately insured up to $250,000 by NCUA and by MSIC for amounts above $250,000.
